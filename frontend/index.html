How It Works

Simplified end-to-end climate resilience pipeline.

🛰️

1. Satellite & Data Fusion

We ingest real-time data from satellites, weather stations, and river gauges across the IGAD region.

🧠

2. AI-Powered Prediction

Our XGBoost models analyze 42+ environmental factors to predict floods and droughts 7 days ahead, down to a 5km radius.

📲

3. Actionable Alerts

We translate probabilities into simple RED/YELLOW/GREEN actions and deliver them via SMS, USSD, Voice, and App in local languages.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AvertAI free for farmers?

Yes, the service is completely free for farmers and pastoralists. Alerts are subsidized by our NGO and Government partners.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our XGBoost models currently operate at an 82% accuracy rate in pilot regions, utilizing the latest Open-Meteo satellite streams.

How do I get alerts in my language?

You can select your preferred language during onboarding via the Mobile App or the USSD menu.
